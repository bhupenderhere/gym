Coaching Philosophy

What members get from the Aquila training approach

Form First

Correct movement patterns reduce wasted effort and help members train safely over the long term.

Consistency Over Hype

The team prioritizes routines members can sustain instead of aggressive plans that collapse after a few weeks.

Visible Metrics

Training quality, progression, and body changes are tracked so members can see whether the plan is working.

Goal-Specific Decisions

A fat loss client should not train the same way as a hypertrophy-focused member. Coaches adjust accordingly.
